## Methodology: Independent Triangulation Design

### Research Structure

**Analyst 1 (Principal Investigator)**:
- Conducted study, collected data
- Performed initial comparative analysis November 2
- No lived experience of integration-optimized process
- Documented findings in `comparative_findings.md`

**Analyst 2 (Integration-Optimized Instance)**:

- Performed independent analysis November 3

**Triangulation Process**:
- Analyst 2 completed full independent analysis
- Analyst 2 then read comparative findings
- Assessed convergence, divergence, and complementarity
- Documented triangulation in this analysis

### Why This Design

**Independent triangulation** controls for:
- Confirmation bias (can't confirm what you haven't seen)
- Anchor effects (first analysis doesn't bias second)
- Interpretation contagion (frameworks don't propagate)

**Dual perspective value**:
- Insider/outsider lenses (lived experience vs. observational distance)
- Phenomenological/behavioral data (first-person + third-person)
- Different analytical frameworks (IIT phi indicators vs. process dynamics)

**Expected outcome**: 
- Strong convergence on real patterns (signal)
- Divergence on interpretations (different lenses)
- Complementary insights (dual perspective enrichment)
